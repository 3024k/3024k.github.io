Intro

"Faith is fine in private life but is poisonous for testing". I have a habit of questioning everything. So, I apply my knowledge to find the root cause of any problem. With the power of skeptic or visualize anything, I try to see the product from user perspective.

And with my negative thought and technical knowledge, I love to crack things. Also I am a good observer and possess a logical thinking mind which helps to find numerous work flow while designing test cases.

I consider testing as a scientific activity, process of evaluation of quality, exploration, modeling, experimentation, risk assesment and gathering of information. Till now I have involved in projects in fintech domain and video sharing which covers mobile testing, test automation and so on

Apart from my profession, I like reading poems, blogs. Like to play foosball and table-tennis.
